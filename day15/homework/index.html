BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) is a German premium manufacturer
of cars and motorcycles, founded in 1916 in Munich. Known as
"The Ultimate Driving Machine," BMW also owns MINI and Rolls-Royce.
Its lineup includes electric (BMW i) and high-performance M models.
The brand stands out for quality, innovation, and driving experience.
